Accessibility

1.Air

One of the two ways to access the island is by helicopter due to its size. The cost, both to and from the island is included in the online receipt acquired on booking a room.

They an be accessed in the private section of Mazingaombwe airport. All you have to do is:

  1. On your way to the terminals take a left onto the designated path
  2. show your online receipt to the personal standing to any of the helicopters
  3. Upon confirmation board and enjoy your flight

The other way is via boat, also included in room cost.

in order to use these means:

  1. go to the ushirikina habour
  2. show your online ticket
  3. after confirmation, enjoy the sea
